Plot Point

Plot Point is a free writing tool to help improve your writing and creativity over time with practice. We challenge you to write a short story every day based on our prompts. Keep writing daily and you will collect a streak! Your old stories can always be found in our history tab. Though that's not all you will find here. We also have a growing assortment of writing tips and ideas for anyone who already is or is interested in writing a novel of their own. Enjoy!
