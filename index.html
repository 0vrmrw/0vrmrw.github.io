Plot Point

Plot Point is a free writing tool to help improve your writing and creativity over time with practice. We challenge you to write a short story everday based on our prompts. Keep writing daily and you will collect a streak! Your old stories can always be found in our history tab. Though that's not all you will find here. We also have a growing assortement of writing tips and ideas for anyone who already is or is interested in writing a novel of their own. Enjoy!





About us

Plot Point was thought up and created by a 14 year old high school student with one goal. Both writing and coding are passions of hers and she wanted to provide others a way to learn writing and help develop creativity all while having fun trying to keep a streak. This was designed specifically with English teachers in mind. It is very simple to use and it's an excellent way to start off English class. A warm up, if you will. Of course, that doesn't mean that only teachers can use Plot Point. Anyone and everyone is welcome to write!
